Calories tracker

Keep track of your daily calorie intake with our user-friendly food logging feature. Simply select the foods you consume throughout the day, and our tracker will automatically calculate your total calorie consumption, helping you stay mindful of your nutritional intake.

User data

Rest assured that your personal data is securely stored in our database, managed with PhpMyAdmin. We prioritize the protection of your information and employ robust security measures to safeguard your privacy and confidentiality.

User notes

Take notes and jot down thoughts, ideas, or reminders to keep yourself organized and motivated. With our user notes feature, you can easily save important information or insights related to your fitness journey, ensuring nothing gets lost along the way.

Workout plan

Follow along with easy-to-follow workout videos designed to help you stay active and healthy. From quick home workouts to beginner-friendly routines, find exercises that fit your schedule and fitness level, making it convenient to prioritize physical activity.

Food recipe

Explore a variety of simple and delicious food recipes to fuel your fitness journey. From nutritious breakfast options to hearty dinners, discover meals that are both satisfying and supportive of your health goals.

Mental advice

Access a curated selection of external resources offering valuable advice and support for mental well-being. Explore articles, guides, and videos covering topics such as stress management, mindfulness, and self-care, provided by trusted sources in the mental health community.
